Rigging and Animation

This is a small selection of stuff I have done for the games I worked on. The rigs and animations are done in blender. The rigs are layered and are both forward and inverse kinematic. The blending spaces are done in UnrealEngine 4. The models were modeled by Stefan Kuzmanovski.
